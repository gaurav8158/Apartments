Overview

Building Dreams, Creating Homes: Our Story

Discover unparalleled luxury and comfort at Prestige Group's upcoming residential project, situated at Prestige City in Siddharth Vihar, Ghaziabad. Spanning across 63 acres and meticulously planned in three phases, this development promises a lifestyle of opulence.

With 80% open green spaces, each 3, 4 & 5 BHK apartment offers park-facing views and finished interiors, ensuring a serene living experience. Boasting direct access from the Delhi Meerut Expressway and just 15 minutes from Akshardham Temple, it seamlessly blends prime location with unmatched connectivity.

Residents will enjoy exclusive amenities including expansive parks, multiple clubhouses, a swimming pool, and a dedicated walking path, making it an ideal choice for those seeking both luxury and convenience in the heart of NCR.

About Developer

The Prestige Group was set up in 1986 and is now the most well-known developer in Bangalore. The group has a wide range of projects in different areas of the country. There are over 200 projects by the group. These are in areas such as Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Mumbai. The group has built villas, apartments, and plots that are known for their high quality. Every home by the builder is designed to be the perfect modern living space. The projects have an array of services that suit all residents. The builder’s projects are in well-connected areas that can be easily reached. These are close to all important services like hospitals and schools. Living in a home by the Prestige Group means living a life of comfort and ease.
